About

Mills PC Club is hosting the first setup competition this year! Throughout the submission phase, PC Club will release examples, guides, and tips to help you improve your setup and gain an edge against your competitors.

Prizes

Prizes are separated into three tiers or levels. As more participants join the competition, another tier is unlocked, gaining access to more prizes and one more winner.

More info about prizes

Rules

  • Participants must be a current Mills student or teacher.
  • An individual must submit their own setup.
  • There may only be one entry per competitor.
  • 1st place winner may choose from the highest unlocked tier and any tiers below.
  • 2nd place winner may only choose from second highest unlocked tier and any tiers below.
  • 3rd place winner may only choose from the lowest tier.
